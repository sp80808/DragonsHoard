## Feature Categories & Sequencing

### Quick Wins (2-3 days each)
1. **Crafting System** - Adds depth to economy
2. **Statistics Dashboard** - Engagement booster
3. **Save Slots** - Quality of life
4. **Undo/Rewind** - Reduces frustration
5. **Mobile Optimization** - Expands audience

**Recommended**: Do all 5 in Phase 1-2 for polish

---

### Medium-Term (3-5 days each)
1. **Daily Challenges** - Engagement loop
2. **Combo System** - Gameplay depth
3. **Tutorial & Onboarding** - Lower barrier to entry
4. **Enhanced Animations** - Polish & feel
5. **Challenge Modes** (Wave/Time Attack) - Replayability
6. **Procedural Content** - Infinite variety

**Recommended**: Prioritize Daily Challenges + Combo for Phase 2

---

### Long-Term (5+ days each)
1. **Story Campaign** - Narrative + investment
2. **Prestige System** - Endgame content
3. **Cosmetics & Skins** - Monetization + expression
4. **Multiplayer** - Community + retention
5. **Guilds/Clans** - Social features
6. **Live Events** - Seasonal engagement

**Recommended**: Space across Phase 4-5 for sustainable development
